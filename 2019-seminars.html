DISTINGUISHED SERVICE AWARD

Georg Riedel, who revolutionized how we drink wine by spreading the gospel of his family’s expertly crafted wineglasses, is the recipient of Wine Spectator’s Distinguished Service Award in 2019. Riedel, 69, is the 10th-generation proprietor of his family’s glass firm, based in Kufstein, Austria. He and his father, Claus, created pioneering wineglasses designed to highlight the aromas and flavors of the wine inside. Riedel has been an active philanthropist, supporting breast cancer charities and Elton John’s AIDS Foundation.
