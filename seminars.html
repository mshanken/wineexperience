2019 Seminar Program

Friday, October 18th — Saturday, October 19th

The Top 10 Wines of 2018

Taste the most exciting wines released in 2018, as selected by Wine Spectator’s editors.

Château Pichon Longueville Lalande

A six-vintage vertical of Château Pichon Longueville Lalande, a leading Second Growth from Pauillac in Bordeaux.

Napa’s Mountain Cabs

Explore the special characteristics and differences of Napa’s “mountain” AVA’s with wines from Mayacamas, Diamond Creek, Dunn and Lokoya.

Bolgheri

A tasting of wines from Bolgheri, a rising star region on the coast of Tuscany.

Chefs’ Challenge

Greats chefs and restaurateurs match food, wine and wits. With José Andrés, Mario Carbone, Emeril Lagasse, and Ti Martin.

Chile

Icon wines from top producers in Chile — a country that has proven their wines rival the best in the world.

Explore the Côte d’Or & Chablis

A selection of grand cru Burgundy wines from the Côte d’Or and Chablis.

A vertical tasting from the leading estate in France’s Châteauneuf-du-Pape.

WINE STARS

Individual presentations of great wines by renowned vintners from around the globe, each a star in the wine world.

Christian Moueix of California's Dominus
