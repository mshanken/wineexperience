Q. What does the Weekend Package include?

A. Friday and Saturday Sit-Down Seminars, Friday and Saturday Lunch, Thursday and Friday Evening Critics’ Choice Grand Tastings, Saturday Evening Grand Award Banquet.

Q. Who can enter the Grand Tasting early at 6:30?

A. Only Weekend Registrants and VIP Ticket Holders.

Q. What time does Grand Tasting General Admission start?

A. 7:30pm on Thursday and Friday evenings.

Q. Do I need to print my ticket?

A. Yes, we suggest that you print your ticket. However, if you have access to your ticket on your mobile device, we can also scan the ticket from there.

Q. Is there a New York Wine Experience App?

A. Yes, you can download the app for iPhone and Android. Enjoy interacting with other wine lovers during the event, plot out which wines you want to taste with the digital map, jot down tasting notes, and check the weekend schedule all in one place!

Q. What is the dress code?

A. Business/Casual for all events, except Grand Award Banquet. The banquet is Black Tie.

Q. Will there be food?

A. Yes, there will be a delicious buffet with a variety of food for you to enjoy while you taste.
