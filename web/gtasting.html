Critics’ Choice
Grand Tastings

Two nights to taste superlative wines!

Thursday, October 20
Friday, October 21

At the evening Critics’ Choice Grand Tastings there will be more than 250 great wineries/châteaus from the world’s best winegrowing regions each presenting one top-rated wine. More than 15 countries will be represented.

All the invited wineries and châteaus at this tasting were selected by the Wine Spectator board of editors and have earned our Critics’ Choice Award. All the wines being poured have been rated 90 points or higher.

This walk-around event is considered one of the most educational and enjoyable features of the weekend. It is a rare opportunity to meet the owners and winemakers who will be pouring their wines.

$325* per person each night.

*Ticket price is $365 after October 10.

7:30 pm is the admission time for people that have purchased Grand Tasting tickets only and are not attending the entire weekend. 6:30 is reserved for Complete Package registrants and guests for whom they have purchased tickets.

For questions email events@mshanken.com

Important Notice: The New York Wine Experience is sponsored by the Winxe Spectator Scholarship Foundation, which underwrites grants and scholarships to students pursuing careers in the wine industry. Attendance is tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

Cancellation Policy: COMPLETE PACKAGE -- A full refund is available until July 1, 2016. After that date, until September 15, there is a $500 per person cancellation fee. After September 15 there are no refunds. GRAND TASTING AND BANQUET TICKETS -- A full refund is available until October 1, 2016. After that date there are no refunds for any tickets. Registrations, Banquet tickets and Grand Tasting tickets may be transferred to another person at any time.

Event sponsor reserves the right to refuse admission to any applicant. Must be 21 or older to attend.
