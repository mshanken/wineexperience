Frequently asked questions

WHAT IS THE WINE SPECTATOR SCHOLARSHIP FOUNDATION?

Education has always been a primary goal of Wine Spectator magazine. Proceeds from the NY Wine Experience support the Wine Spectator Scholarship Foundation, which provides grants and scholarships to a wide variety of wine-related and culinary programs. To date, more than $30 million has been raised, made possible by the generosity of vintners around the world, who donate the wine for the Wine Experience Grand Tastings and seminars, as well as the thousands of wine lovers who attend the event each year.

 

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

Business casual for all events.

 

WHAT ARE THE HEALTH AND SAFETY REQUIREMENTS?

While there are no required health and safety protocols for the New York Wine Experience (proof of vaccination is not required to attend), however as a courtesy to all of those attending, we request that all attendees please consider the following

  • Please consider taking a COVID-19 rapid at-home test the day before the event.
  • If you have a positive result, we respectfully request that you do not attend the event. Questions regarding a positive test result can be directed to events@mshanken.com.
  • If you wish to wear a mask and have forgotten one we will be supplying them at Will Call upon request.
