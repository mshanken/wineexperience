Frequently asked questions

GENERAL EVENT QUESTIONS

 

WHAT IS THE WINE SPECTATOR SCHOLARSHIP FOUNDATION?

Education has always been a primary goal of Wine Spectator magazine. Proceeds from the NY Wine Experience support the Wine Spectator Scholarship Foundation, which provides grants and scholarships to a wide variety of wine-related and culinary programs. To date, more than $20 million has been raised, made possible by the generosity of vintners around the world, who donate the wine for the Wine Experience Grand Tastings and seminars, as well as the thousands of wine lovers who attend  the event each year.

 

Most recently, the foundation made a $250,000 donation to José Andrés’ charitable

organization, World Central Kitchen, to feed and support front-line health care workers

and Americans who have lost their jobs due to COVID-19 including restaurant workers.

 

WHAT DOES A COMPLETE WEEKEND PACKAGE INCLUDE?

Admission for one person to: Friday and Saturday seated daytime wine seminars, Friday and Saturday wine-pairing luncheons, and Private Early Access entry for Thursday and Friday evening Critics’ Choice Grand tastings. You must be 21 or older to attend.

 

Please note: We have made the difficult decision to cancel the Grand Award Banquet in 2021. The NY Wine Experience will end after the conclusion of the Saturday afternoon seminar program.

 

WHEN DO CRITICS’ CHOICE GRAND TASTING TICKETS GO ON SALE?

Individual tickets for the Critics’ Choice Grand Tastings will go on sale in SEPTEMBER 2021. There will be no VIP tickets available for purchase this year. Only General Admission tickets (8:00-10:00pm) will be available for purchase. We will share more information prior to ticket launch. You must be 21 or older to attend.

 

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

Business casual for all events.

 

CAN I PURCHASE AN INDIVIDUAL TICKET TO ATTEND A SEMINAR?

Seminars are only included in the Weekend Package. Individual seminar tickets are not available for purchase.

 

WILL THERE BE FOOD AT THE CRITICS’ CHOICE GRAND TASTINGS?

Yes, there will be a light buffet (food will be served by a chef attendant) during the tasting on the 5th and 6th floor.

 

WHEN WILL THE LIST OF WINERIES POURING BE POSTED?

We plan to share the list of participating wineries later this summer.

 

WILL THERE BE A TASTING MAP FOR THE GRAND TASTINGS?

Yes, we will share the tasting map when the event gets closer.

 

 

COVID-19 QUESTIONS & REGISTRATION TRANSFERS FROM 2020

 

WHAT PRECAUTIONS WILL THE EVENT BE TAKING TO ENSURE THE HEALTH AND SAFETY OF ATTENDEES?

Proof of COVID-19 vaccination is now required to attend the 2021 New York Wine Experience. A negative PCR test is no longer sufficient for admission.

In addition, we will be reducing the number of Weekend Packages and Grand Tasting tickets sold to ensure a less crowded environment. More details about vaccination/PCR test verification and additional precautions being taken by the event and the Marriott Marquis will be shared in September.

 

I TRANSFERRED MY 2020 REGISTRATION TO 2021. HOW DO I FIND MY NEW REGISTRATION?

If you previously registered for the 2020 event, you should have received a new confirmation email for the 2021 event from Wine Spectator (events@mshanken.com) with a new Registration Confirmation for the 2021 event. Please check your inbox and spam folder. If you still have trouble locating your new registration confirmation email, please email us at events@mshanken.com. We will be happy to assist you.
