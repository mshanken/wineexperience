Seminar Program

 

Château Haut-Brion

A vertical tasting from the renowned Bordeaux first growth, spanning 15 years and culminating with the estate’s rare white wine.

 

Biondi-Santi

A vertical tasting from the leading estate in Tuscany’s Brunello di Montalcino back to the 1983 Riserva.

 

Burgundy Grand Cru

Wines from the Corton grand cru vineyard: four different vintages from four of Burgundy’s top producers.

 

Schrader Cellars

Founder Fred Schrader and senior editor James Laube moderate a tasting of six Cabernets from the highly-regarded Beckstoffer To Kalon vineyard in Napa Valley.

 

Great Chardonnay from Australia

There’s more than Shiraz “Down Under". Leading estates present their elegant, complex and lively Chardonnays.

 

Oregon’s Rising Stars

Four rising star estates explore the state’s distinctive terroirs and showcase the extraordinary 2015 vintage.

 

Wine Stars

Individual presentations of great wines by renowned vintners from around the globe, each a star in the wine world.

● Cristie Kerr, pro golfer and owner of Kerr Cellars

● Laura Catena, of Argentina’s Bodegas Catena Zapata

● Priscilla Incisa della Rocchetta, of Tenuta San Guido in Italy’s Bolgheri region, producer of Sassicaia all present an exceptional wine.

● Mark Aubert of California’s Aubert Wines

● Nik Weis of St. Urbans Hof

 

Chefs’ Challenge

Great chefs and restaurateurs match food, wine and wits. Four dishes, eight wines — you decide which wines pair best. A weekend favorite!
